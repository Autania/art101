Lab 4- Pseudocode and problem solving

  • Identify what you would like to paint
  • Gather supplies
  • Colored paint, Paint brushes, Canvas of any size, Easel, Paint Pallet, Water washing cups, Smock, varnish
  • Go to location
  • Set up Easel and canvas
  • Put on smock to protect your clothes from paint
  • Identify colors of paint you will use to replicate the scene you want to paint
  • Squeeze paint on to pallet in small dollops
  • Use brush to apply paint to canvas
  • pply background colors first let paint dry between layers
  • Wash brush in water before using a new color to prevent cross contamination
  • Layer paint to mimic scenery
  • Copy what you see at location in your own individual art style
  • Paint small details over large details
  • Add lighter colored paints as highlights -Admire your painting as it drys -Add your own signature -After painting is completely dry add varnish if you want. -Wash brushes, remove smock, pack up supplies -Take home painting -Hang it up on wall or give to a friend.
